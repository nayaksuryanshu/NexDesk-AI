# Billing and Payments Guide

## Payment services

NexDesk AI supports the following payment services for customer purchases and subscriptions:

- Credit and debit cards through major card networks
- PayPal for quick checkout
- Apple Pay for supported devices and browsers
- Manual invoicing for enterprise customers when enabled

## How payment is verified

Payments are verified automatically through the payment processor at checkout. The system checks:

- Card validity
- Available funds or authorization
- Billing address consistency where required
- Fraud and risk signals from the payment processor

If verification fails, the customer should try a different payment method or contact their bank.

## Common billing questions

### Why was my payment declined?

A payment can be declined for several reasons:

- Incorrect card number, expiry date, or CVV
- Insufficient funds
- Bank fraud protection
- Billing address mismatch
- Temporary processor or network issue

### Can I get a receipt or invoice?

Yes. Receipts are generated after successful payment. Enterprise customers can request invoices for accounting and procurement purposes.

### Can I change my payment method?

Yes. Customers can update their payment method before the next billing cycle or before placing a new order.

### Do you store card details?

No raw card details are not stored by NexDesk AI. Payment data is handled by secure third-party payment processors, and sensitive information is encrypted in transit.

### What if I was charged twice?

If a duplicate charge appears, contact support with the transaction details. The support team will verify the charge and help issue a correction if needed.

### How are refunds handled?

Refunds follow the 30-day money-back policy. Once approved, the refund is processed back to the original payment method.

## Subscription and account billing

- Plans can be upgraded or downgraded based on account permissions.
- Billing changes usually take effect on the next cycle unless otherwise noted.
- Enterprise billing may include custom terms, usage-based invoicing, or a dedicated account manager.

## Support contact for billing

If the user asks about payment services, verification, receipts, refunds, or failed transactions, the support reply should reference this guide along with the company FAQ and support policy documents.
